History

Journey Through Memories is the brainchild of our creative director, Niken Hertanto. Inspired by The Last Guardian, she created a world of secrets and a dark past. Given the chance to make this world into reality in UC Santa Cruz Games and Playable Media program, Team ._.)b formed to bring life into this game.

Wanting players to feel like a powerful wizard or a magical witch, Niken wanted players to use their voice in order to cast spells to solve puzzles and to make discoveries in the mysterious world. With this in mind, we used speech recognition technology through Microsoft Cortana to capture the voice of the players. Seeing this potential, we want to use this technology to allow those with disabilities to be able to play and enjoy games like ours.

Description

Using just the limitless power of your voice, guide a young witch named Vivi as she travels through a magical realm of lingering spirits and a buried past. As the ancient aftermath of a devastating war continues to take its toll, use Vivi's magical abilities to aid these fallen victims in an effort to finally restore peace.

Set in a hand-painted storybook world, discover and cast various spells to overcome obstacles. Encounter the forgotten spirits and fulfill their last requests before guiding them into the afterlife. Find the untold stories and journey through the memories of the inhabitants to piece together the hidden truth behind history's darkest days. In a game where discovery is just as important as puzzle solving, can you help Vivi in her quest to right an ancient wrong?

Game Features

  • Speech Recognition : Use your voice to control Vivi. Cast spells and move her towards her goals.
  • Spells : Discover 120+ spells each with their own abilities and visuals.
  • Puzzles : Solve various obstacles, complete spirit challenges, and collect hidden items with the spells you've discovered.
  • Hand-painted artwork : Watercolored environments and characters set in a hauntingly beautiful world.
  • Visually stunning effects : Sparkles and shine, and everything nice.
  • Music : Soothing and original soundtrack
  • Narrative-Driven : Sit back and relax as the story unfolds.

Team ._.)b (pronounced dot-dot-b) is an independent team whose goal is to find alternative ways to play video games that doesn't require a controller or a keyboard while having our players explore a world of mystery and lore. As a greenlit project at UC Santa Cruz's Games and Playable Media program, Journey Through Memories takes you on a whimsical journey through a lost city with a hidden past. All using the power of your voice to overcome each puzzle and challenge.
